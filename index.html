Evolution Championship Series 2024

What is Evolution 2024?

Evolution, typically called Evo, is the largest and most well known annual open-bracket Fighting Game Tournament in the world. It consist of many different video games, such as Street Fighter 6 and Tekken 8.

What's so important about Evo?

Everyone has their own hobbies and personal interest. Video games are a very popular one and the fighting game genre, specifically, is one of the reasons why games are so popular today. At Evo, the entire community comes out to celebrate the genre and what is has meant to them each indivdually. This year, Evo reached 10,240 indivdual entrants for all of it's games. That's an 11.52% increase from the previous year and it is bound to keep growing. Not only that but players from around the world join in on the games, with 64 different countries signing up to attend.

Not only do people come out to Evo for cash rewards and bragging rights, but they also come for Evo itself. The Tournament has become more than just that but also a convention of everything fighting games. Game Studios come through to show what their development teams are cooking up, museums are setup to show the history of fighting (the arcadestick museum was the best), entrants can cosplay as their favorite characters, tons of vendors to purchase merch from titles that are loved, and more. It's become not only a tournament, but a convention.

Why Evo?

I have been playing fighting games since 2014, so it's been 10 years at this point that I have been apart of this community. If I am being honest, I have never been to Evo. However, it's been a dream of mine. I've watched it every year on Twitch and even once on ESPN (that was crazy to see). And even then, I never fail to become emotional watching this thing. I don't want to go pro at video games, I don't want to cash rewards or anything like that. I just want to celebrate something that has kept me going ever since I was a lost high school kid. Also, this thing went from supporting itself to being sponsored by Chipotle! I got three free burritos!
